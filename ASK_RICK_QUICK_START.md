## What Can You Ask?

Just type these words and press Enter:

### Quick Info
- **status** - How's the system doing?
- **health** - Is everything working?
- **trading** - Am I trading right now?

### Money Stuff
- **balance** - Show my accounts
- **trades** - What trades happened?
- **positions** - What's open now?

### Details
- **brokers** - Which brokers am I using?
- **activity** - What's happening?
- **features** - What can this system do?

### Help
- **help** - Show me what I can ask
- **menu** - Show all commands
- **quit** - I'm done
